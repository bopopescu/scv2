Description of the website

  • Searching for a good deal?

    You are in the right website! Here are reviews of many movies, TV series, books, and so on.

  • Sign in

    Don't hesitate to sign in if you would like to participate to the website's community. Feel free to express yourself, give your opinion as much as you can, and share same interests with other users.

  • Give your score!

    Give a review to an item, between 0 and 5 stars. The more you give reviews, the more reliable is the website!

  • Give your opinion!

    Do not hesitate to give your review from a constructive point of view in order to defend the score that you are giving to the chosen item. By this way, the other users can rely to your point of view if they have to make a choice (for a restaurant, a smartphone, ...).

  • Discover!

    Thanks to the search bar, you can search some content thanks to the keywords you put in. Want some Tim Burton movies ? Just write down his name in the search bar and enjoy ! If you ever don't have any idea of what kind of review you are interested in, the scrolling bar at the left can help.

  • Share

    Share the website on your social networks in order to increase your community and to get more and more reviews on many items!
